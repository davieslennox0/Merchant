Privacy Policy

Merchant Agent — last updated 17 July 2026

What we store, and where

  • Invoices, payments, ledger — stored only in your device's browser local storage. They never leave your device and we cannot see them.
  • Wallet address — read from your wallet to display your balance and watch for incoming payments. It is sent to the public Celo Sepolia RPC as part of normal blockchain queries and is never stored on our server.
  • Assistant messages — when you chat with the in-app assistant, your message plus summary business totals (e.g. income/expense numbers) are sent to our server and forwarded to the Groq API to generate a reply. We do not keep a database of your conversations.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no sign-up, no KYC, no cookies, no analytics trackers.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with anyone.
  • We never access your private keys or funds.

Third parties

The app talks to: the Celo Sepolia RPC (blockchain queries), the Blockscout explorer (links you tap), the Groq API (assistant replies, via our server), and WhatsApp (only if you tap "Share on WhatsApp"). Each is governed by its own privacy policy.

Data removal

Clearing the Mini App's site data in MiniPay/your browser permanently deletes all locally stored records.
