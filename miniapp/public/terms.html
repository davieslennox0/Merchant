Merchant Agent — last updated 17 July 2026

1. What this app is

Merchant Agent is a demonstration Mini App built for the Celo Agentic Payments & DeFAI Hackathon. It runs on the Celo Sepolia test network only. All balances, invoices, and payments use testnet cUSD, which has no monetary value.

2. Non-custodial

The app never holds, controls, or has access to your funds or private keys. Every transaction is constructed locally and signed by you in your own wallet (MiniPay). The in-app assistant can only propose actions; nothing is executed without your explicit wallet confirmation.

3. Your data

Invoices and ledger entries are stored only in your device's local storage. Messages you send to the assistant are processed by a third-party language-model API to generate a reply and are not used to build a profile of you. See the Privacy Policy.
